⚙️ Phase 1: Foundation & Visualization (Start Small)

🗓️ Estimated Duration: 4Q 2025 → mid-1Q 2026

 

🎯 Objective: Produce leadership-ready analytics and visuals directly from MineralWare dataturning raw statements into findings, recommendations, and clarity that immediately enhance reporting confidence.

 

📦 Deliverables

 

-        Monthly and Quarterly Summary Packets

-        12-page visual overviews highlighting trends, anomalies, and actionable metrics

-        Quarterly 48 page detailed packets for Hollis, Houston, and specified entities

-        Whats Missing Analysis surfacing unpaid or underpaid wells and revenue discrepancies

-        Statement Normalization unifying well names, operator nomenclature, and product taxonomy

-        Quarterly Newsletter-Style Reports expanding in depth and design over time

-        Analytics & Charts via PDF or PowerPoint, professionally formatted and optionally white-labeled under the Veritas brand

-        Supporting Elements: preliminary ERP-upload templates and light collaboration with land teams to ensure property naming consistency

 

💼 Cadence & Delivery

 

-        Monthly refreshes managed by Stewardship.IS

-        Secure delivery via email or shared workspace

-        Portal notifications and automation introduced later once the hosted environment is ready

 

💡 Immediate Value Add

 

-        Transforms data into concise findings and recommendations for management and boards

-        Applies 35 proprietary formulas to identify missing revenue and incorrect deductionscreating early ROI without system changes

-        Reinforces Veritass professional image through high-impact, client-facing deliverables

 



🌐 Phase 2: Hybrid Portal Mode

🗓️ Estimated Duration: 1Q 2026 → 2Q 2026

 

🎯 Objective: Evolve from static deliverables to a secure, interactive portal blending MineralWare data with Stewardship.IS normalization, analytics, and ongoing findingsproviding continuous clarity and measurable improvement opportunities.

 

📦 Deliverables

 

-        Online Portal Access for Veritas and approved stakeholders

-        Expanded visual dashboards covering normalized data, missing wells, deductions, and LOS metrics

-        Comparative Analytics and Anomaly Highlights across operators and basins

-        Board-Ready Visualization Layouts exportable for presentations and investor updates

-        Supporting Elements: collaborative refinement of ERP upload templates and integration touchpoints with Veritas accounting and land teams

-        Initial mapping visualizations (limited) basic shapefile overlays supplied by Veritas; full mapping capabilities arrive in Phase 3

 

💼 Cadence & Delivery

 

-        Monthly portal refresh with optional quarterly commentary

-        Role-based access managed by Stewardship.IS

 

💡 Value Add

 

-        Keeps MineralWare fully active while layering S.IS analytics and findings on topno operational disruption

-        Expands the number of proprietary formulas running in the background (typically four to five) to capture recurring anomalies and cross-operator outliers

-        Delivers a living reporting environment that turns data into answers and strategic insight

 

🚀 Phase 3: Full Migration to Stewardship.IS

🗓️ Estimated Duration: 68 months after Phase 1 kickoff (projected late 3Q 2026 onward)

 

🎯 Objective: Transition to a comprehensive Stewardship.IS environment for data ingestion, normalization, analytics, and reportingestablishing a long-term framework for answers and continuous value capture.

 

📦 Deliverables

 

-        All revenue and JIB statements processed through the S.IS normalization pipeline (PDF + Excel)

-        Full analytics suite of Stewardship.IS deliverables including Deduct Defender, Landman Cheat Sheet, LOS & Exception Reports

-        ERP-ready uploads provided for Veritass existing systems (WolfePak and others) to streamline monthly close processes

-        Ongoing collaboration with Veritas accounting and land teams to ensure alignment and data integrity

-        Client & Investor Portals expanded for on-demand dashboards and investor-grade findings

-        Full mapping functionality and advanced geospatial overlays (graduating from Phase 2s initial mapping visualizations)

 

💡 Value Add

 

-        Integrates the full family of Stewardship.IS formulas and analytics to recover missed revenue and quantify inefficiencies

-        Converts every dataset into recommendations and action plans that strengthen returns and governance visibility

-        Positions Veritas with a scalable, tech-driven framework for continuous performance improvement
